Tesla tops brand loyalty rating for the first time

5 March 2023

2354

Share it

Tesla has surpassed Ford for the first time in the number of wins at the Automotive Loyalty Awards. In total, the brand managed to win in five out of eight nominations.

The Americans have determined the winners of the Automotive Loyalty Awards 2022. The Tesla brand received the most awards. This was reported on the Autoevolution website.

Tesla often finds itself at the center of various scandals. Often they are associated with poor build quality of electric vehicles or various frauds. Nevertheless, the brand managed to win a huge share of satisfied customers.

Tesla has won five of the eight Manufacturer and Make Loyalty Awards. This year, for the first time, the brand surpassed Ford in terms of customer loyalty in the US. At this time, General Motors leads the category "general loyalty to the automaker."

Experts noted that in 2022, due to the shortage of cars, many customers chose models from other manufacturers. Therefore, the average loyalty to certain brands decreased from 54.6% in 2019 to 50.2%

Tesla Model 3 wins "Best Premium Compact Car" category
Photo: Tesla

Tesla received the "General Loyalty to Manufacturing" award for the first time. Prior to that, Ford held the prize for more than a decade in a row.

Tesla also won in the "Highest Market Conquest Percentage", "Best Alternative Vehicle Loyalty" and "Enhanced Loyalty" nominations. The Model 3 won the Best Premium Compact Car category and the Model Y won the Best Premium Compact Crossover category.
