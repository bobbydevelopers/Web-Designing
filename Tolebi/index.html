The Way of Abai

Mukhtar Auezov

Historical Adventure Classic

The epic "The Way of Abai" by the famous Soviet writer, laureate of the Lenin Prize M. Auezov (1897-1961) is dedicated to the life and work of the great Kazakh poet and educator Abai Kunanbayev. In the first volume of the novel, against the backdrop of a vivid picture of the life of the Kazakh people in the second half of the 19th century, it tells about the childhood and youth of the hero, about the formation of his views, his birth and development as a poet.F
